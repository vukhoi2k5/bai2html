scramb led eggs

eggs are one of my favorite foods , here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • itbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter hass melted add cream and eggs
  4. usong a sptula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it wiill continue to cook on the plate until served)
