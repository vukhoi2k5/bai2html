scambled eggs

eggs are one of my favorite foods . here is s recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

ingredintts

  • 2 eggs
  • itbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

method

  1. melt butter in a fry pan over a medium heat
  2. gantly mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from thr heat
